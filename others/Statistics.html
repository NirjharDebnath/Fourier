  • Using The Regression Formula

$$ b_{xy} = \frac{n \sum (XY) - \sum (X)\sum (Y)}{n\sum (X^2) - \sum(X)^2}, b_{yx} = \frac{n \sum (XY) - \sum (X)\sum (Y)}{n\sum (Y^2) - \sum(Y)^2} $$
